📚 Study Focus AI

Study Focus AI is an intelligent study planning and productivity web application that helps students manage their study time efficiently. It allows users to create tasks, track study sessions, and generate AI-based weekly study schedules to stay organized and productive.

🚀 Features

🧠 AI Study Planner – Generates personalized weekly study schedules.

✅ Task Management – Add, track, and manage study tasks.

⏱ Study Timer – Track focused study sessions.

📊 Analytics Dashboard – View progress, completion rate, and study time.

🔥 Streak Tracking – Encourages consistent learning habits.

📅 Weekly Schedule View – Visual calendar for planning study sessions.
